Spatial Accessibility Comparer

This web application allows users to upload two GeoPackage files, visualize them on a basemap, and compute spatial accessibility measures such as 2SFCA and gravity-based accessibility. Users can select fields from each layer to use in the accessibility calculations, and the results are displayed as new layers on the map.

Step 1: Load Data

Users can either load their own GeoPackage files or use the provided example data for Cook County hospitals and census tracts.

No data? Use the example dataset (Cook County hospitals and census tracts).

Step 2: Compute Accessibility

After loading the data, users can calculate accessibility. The results will be displayed as new layers on the map, and legends will be provided for interpretation.

2sfca results

Step 3: Examine Results

Click anywhere on the map to explore accessibility classes at specific locations. Use the legends to interpret how accessibility varies across the study area under different methods. You can drag and rearrange the legend panel to change the layer order.
